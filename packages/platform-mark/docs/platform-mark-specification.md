# BrianShortApps Platform Mark Specification

## Status

Master geometry pending

## Purpose

The Platform Mark is the canonical symbol of the BrianShortApps ecosystem.

It must be engineered as precise vector geometry, not traced from raster or AI-generated concept art.

## Master Canvas

- Canvas: 1000 × 1000
- Center: 500, 500

## Ring Model

- Outer Ring: Experience
- Middle Ring: Products
- Inner Ring: Progress

## Construction Requirements

- Outer ring breaks at 12, 4, and 8 o'clock
- Middle ring breaks only at midpoint positions: 2, 6, and 10 o'clock
- Inner ring breaks at 12, 4, and 8 o'clock
- Inner ring includes one-sided bevels to imply subtle inward motion
- Product-safe center zone remains visually dominant
- Rings support; application leads

## Source of Truth

The master SVG is the canonical asset.

All derived outputs must come from the master geometry.
