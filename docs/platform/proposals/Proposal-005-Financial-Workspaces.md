# Proposal 005 — Financial Workspaces

**Status:** Draft  
**Sprint:** Sprint Two — First Private Application

---

## Purpose

Financial Workspaces are focused operating areas that help the user understand and act on specific portions of Financial State.

Workspaces do not own calculations.

Workspaces present, explain, and help the user act.

---

## Core Principle

Every workspace exists to answer one primary financial question.

---

## Financial Snapshot Workspace

Primary question:

What is financially true right now?

Owns presentation of:

- Current liquidity
- Upcoming obligations
- Credit health
- Budget status
- Net worth
- Financial health
- Recommended next actions

---

## Income Workspace

Primary question:

What resources entered or are expected to enter my financial life?

Presents:

- Paychecks
- Income sources
- Pay periods
- Gross pay
- Net pay
- Deductions
- Paycheck variance
- Income trend

---

## Accounts Workspace

Where does my money live and where do I owe money?

- Institutions
- Accounts
- Credit limits
- Balances

- Open dates

## Financial Intelligence Workspace

- Debt progress
- Net worth history

---

## Workspace Rule

A workspace may trigger a new Financial Event through user action.

A workspace may not directly recalculate Financial State.

---

## Product Promise

The user should never need to mentally assemble their financial picture from disconnected screens.

Each workspace presents one coherent view of the same Financial State.
