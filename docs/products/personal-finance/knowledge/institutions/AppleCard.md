## Rules Discovered

### Grace Period

Apple Card states that interest can be avoided by paying the Monthly Balance as of the end of last month in full by the payment due date this month.

### Daily Balance Method

Apple Card calculates interest using the daily balance method, including new transactions.

### Payment Posting

Electronic payments made by 11:59 PM Eastern time are credited on the payment date.
