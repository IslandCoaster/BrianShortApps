# Daily Interest Accrual

## Purpose

Calculate interest accrued during a single accounting day.

---

## Owner

Interest Engine

---

## Inputs

- Daily Ledger Closing Balance
- APR
- Grace Period State

---

## Formula

Daily Interest

=

Closing Balance

×

APR

÷

365

---

## Product Principle

Daily Interest is calculated independently for every accounting day.

Statement Interest is the sum of Daily Interest across the statement period.
