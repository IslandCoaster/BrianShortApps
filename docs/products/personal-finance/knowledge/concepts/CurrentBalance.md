# Current Balance

## Definition

The live amount currently owed on an account after all posted transactions, payments, credits, fees, and interest.

Current Balance changes continuously throughout the statement cycle.

---

## Characteristics

- Mutable
- Operational
- Real-time
- Continuously recalculated

---

## Owner

Account State Engine

---

## Used By

- Credit Position Engine
- Debt Position Engine
- Financial Snapshot
- Recommendation Engine

---

## Related Concepts

- Statement Balance
- Projected Statement Balance
- Reported Balance
- Available Credit

---

## Notes

Current Balance is not historical.

It represents the account balance at this moment in time.
