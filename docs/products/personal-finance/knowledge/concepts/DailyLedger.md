# Daily Ledger

## Definition

The collection of financial activity that occurred during a single accounting day.

---

## Components

Opening Balance

Purchases

Payments

Fees

Interest

Closing Balance

---

## Owner

Daily Balance Engine

---

## Accounting Invariant

Opening Balance

plus Purchases

plus Fees

plus Interest

minus Payments

equals Closing Balance

---

## Product Principle

The Closing Balance of one day becomes the Opening Balance of the next day.

## Adjustments

Adjustments represent balance-affecting activity that is not a purchase, payment, fee, or interest.

Examples:

- Refunds
- Credits
- Disputes
- Manual corrections
- Promotional credits
