# Statement Period

## Definition

The inclusive accounting period represented by a statement.

Example:

June 1, 2026 through June 30, 2026.

---

## Characteristics

- Historical
- Immutable
- Date range
- Defines the accounting period represented by a statement

---

## Owner

- Obligation State Engine
- Interest Engine

---

## Related Concepts

- Statement Closing Date
- Payment Due Date
- Reporting Date
- Statement Balance
- Interest Period

---

## Product Principle

Statement Period is not Statement Closing Date.

Statement Closing Date is a point in time.

Statement Period is a range of time.

Every statement has exactly one Statement Period.
