# Daily Balance

## Definition

The balance at the end of a single accounting day.

Daily Balance is generated by replaying account activity across a statement period.

---

## Characteristics

- Date-specific
- Generated
- Historical for closed periods
- Projected for future periods
- Consumed by interest calculations

---

## Owner

Daily Balance Engine

---

## Used By

- Interest Engine
- Average Daily Balance
- Forecast Engine
- Financial Sandbox
- Payment Simulation

---

## Product Principle

The Interest Engine does not maintain balances.

The Interest Engine consumes Daily Balances produced by the Daily Balance Engine.
