# Projected Statement Balance

## Definition

The balance expected to appear on the next statement if no additional financial activity occurs before statement closing.

---

## Characteristics

- Forecast
- Mutable
- Scenario-dependent

---

## Owner

Credit Position Engine

---

## Used By

- Projected Utilization
- Recommendation Engine
- Forecast Engine
- Credit Position

---

## Related Concepts

- Statement Balance
- Current Balance
- Reported Balance

---

## Notes

Projected Statement Balance changes whenever:

- New transactions occur
- Payments are made
- Credits are applied
- Fees are assessed
- Interest is accrued

It is never historical.
