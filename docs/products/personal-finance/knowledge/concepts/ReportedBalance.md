# Reported Balance

## Definition

The balance most recently reported to a credit bureau by the financial institution.

Reported Balance may differ from:

- Current Balance
- Projected Statement Balance
- Statement Balance

---

## Characteristics

- Historical
- External
- Institution-controlled

---

## Owner

Future Credit Reporting Engine

---

## Used By

- Credit Score Correlation
- Credit History
- Reporting Analysis

---

## Related Concepts

- Statement Balance
- Current Balance
- Projected Statement Balance

---

## Notes

Credit bureaus typically evaluate Reported Balance rather than Current Balance.

The timing of reporting depends on the institution's reporting practices and is not guaranteed to coincide with the statement closing date.
