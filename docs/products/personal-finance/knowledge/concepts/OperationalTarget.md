# Operational Target

## Definition

A configurable operating objective used by Position Engines to guide financial recommendations.

Operational Targets represent preferred operating conditions.

They are not legal limits.

They are not guarantees.

---

## Examples

### Credit

29%

### Ideal Credit

9%

### Emergency Fund

6 Months

### Retirement Contribution

15%

### Debt Free Date

User Defined

---

## Product Principle

Recommendations should attempt to move the user toward Operational Targets.

Operational Targets are configurable by the user and may change over time.
