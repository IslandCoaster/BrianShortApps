# Available Credit

## Definition

The unused revolving credit currently available for additional purchases.

---

## Formula

Available Credit

=

Credit Limit

−

Current Balance

---

## Characteristics

- Current
- Operational
- Continuously changing

---

## Owner

Credit Position Engine

---

## Used By

- Credit Position
- Recommendation Engine
- Credit Health

---

## Related Concepts

- Credit Limit
- Current Balance
- Utilization

---

## Notes

Available Credit is an operational value.

It is not reported to credit bureaus.
